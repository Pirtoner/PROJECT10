Welcome to My Movie Website

Welcome! This website explores how sometimes movies are more than just entertainment. Each of the next movie pages looks at important themes, characters, and lessons that can be applied to real life.

Purpose of This Website

The goal of this website is to introduce you to the selected films and show the messages they try to convey. By studying movies like Parasite, Knives Out,Shawshank Redemption andSpider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse we can see how storytelling reflects real-world issues, human behavior and how we can learn from movies.

How Movies Teach Life Lessons

Movies can teach us a lot of life lessons, from morality to personal growth, and from hope to confidence, ambition, and kindness. They show us different perspectives, challenge our thinking, and help us understand topics such as class differences, integrity, and survival. Through film, we learn lessons that can influence how we see the world and help us grow.
