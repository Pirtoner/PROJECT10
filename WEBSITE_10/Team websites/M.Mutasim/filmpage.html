Movie Summary

Released in 1994 and directed by Frank Darabont, The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully sentenced to life in Shawshank Prison. The film explores hope, friendship, and personal resilience in the darkest of times.

Main Character I Admire

Andy Dufresne symbolizes hope, intelligence, and moral resilience. Despite decades in prison, he maintains faith, inspires others, and never compromises his integrity.
