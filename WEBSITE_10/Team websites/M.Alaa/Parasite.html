Parasite (2019)

A Dark Comedy Thriller About Class and Survival

Movie Summary

Parasite is a South Korean movie published in 2019 that mixes dark comedy with drama and suspense. It follows the Kim family, who live in extreme poverty, as they cleverly infiltrate the wealthy Park household by posing as highly qualified and unrelated workers.

The story begins when Ki-woo, the eldest son of the Kim family, takes his friend’s job as an English tutor for the Park daughter. From there, the Kim family slowly replaces the existing workers in the Park home, building trust with the rich family while hiding their true relationship.

This movie explores how social class, greed, and survival instincts can shape human behavior.

Topics Discussed & Lessons Learned

  • The Difference Between Classes
    • Wealth can create emotional distance and ignorance
    • The large gap between the rich and the poor
    • How class affects family structure and opportunities
  • Greed and Survival
    • Hard work alone does not always guarantee success
    • Poverty can force morally difficult decisions
    • Money strongly influences relationships
